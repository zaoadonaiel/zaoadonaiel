Our Story

A Legacy of
Faith & Love

Founded in 1968 by Pastor James & Eleanor Harris, Grace Cathedral began as a small congregation of 12 families meeting in a living room. Today, we are a thriving church family of over 3,000 members, united by one vision: to love God, love people, and transform our city.

We believe the Church is not a building — it's a people. Every Sunday, we gather to encounter the living God and scatter throughout the week to be His hands and feet in our community.
